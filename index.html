Kevin, Ivory Coast:

Ivory Coast, also known as Côte d’Ivoire, is located in West Africa along the Atlantic Ocean. It is known for its vibrant culture, diverse landscapes, and rich biodiversity. The country is the world’s largest producer of cocoa beans and is famous for its stunning beaches, tropical forests, and lively cities like Abidjan and Yamoussoukro. Ivory Coast is also home to unique wildlife and several UNESCO World Heritage sites, making it a fascinating destination to explore.
