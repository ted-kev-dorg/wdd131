AfroBasket Store

Bringing African's Best to Your Basket

a) Site Name

Site Name: AfroBasket Store
Description: The name "AfroBasket Store" reflects the store’s focus on authentic African groceries and products, making it easy for customers to identify the store’s specialty and cultural roots. The name is short, memorable, and suitable for branding.
Optional domain: afro-basket.com

b) Site Purpose

The purpose of AfroBasket Store is to provide a welcoming online presence for a local African grocery store, showcasing a diverse range of authentic African food, beverages, and household products. The site will offer information about the store, highlight featured products, and allow customers to browse categories, learn about African cuisine, and contact the store for inquiries or orders. The website aims to promote cultural diversity and make it easier for the community to access Afrocentric products.

c) Scenarios

  • Which African food staples and spices are available at AfroBasket Store?
  • How can I contact the store or place an order for pickup?
  • Are there any special deals or new arrivals this week?

d) Color Schema

  • Deep Green ➽ #1e5631: Used for headings, navigation bar, and logo background.
  • Warm Gold ➽ #f9a602: Used for buttons, highlights, and accent elements.
  • Light Cream ➽ #fff8e1: Used for page background to create a warm, inviting feel.
  • Dark Text ➽ #222: Used for main body text for readability.

e) Typography

  • Montserrat (Google Font): Used for all headings and the logo for a bold, modern look.
  • Roboto (Google Font): Used for body text and navigation for clarity and readability.
